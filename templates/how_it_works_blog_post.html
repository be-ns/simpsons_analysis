VeloSpark and Intro to Recommender Systems

by Gordon MacMillan

Posted on March 5, 2017 at 5:31 PM

Now nearing the end of my data science curriculum with Galvanize, I have learned models for regression and classification as well as completing the trifecta to learn recommender systems. Being an avid cyclist and having a strong background in exploring the colorado outdoors, I thought what better topic to complete my project on than cycling.

Once I looked at the data, it made sense to apply recommendations to see if I could find neat rides in the state of Colorado.
